Scenario

What the database manages

The system supports an esports organization that hosts tournaments for games such as Valorant, DOTA 2, Counter Strike, Apex Legends, COD, Delta Force, PUBG, and Football.

Players register once, teams are created independently, registrations connect teams with tournament matches, matches record competing teams and scores, and prizes are assigned to tournament positions.
